Glow

In the process of designing glow web app, I used a process similar to design thinking but made use of other standard best practices like Lean UX, UCD and Double Diamond. Tools used to design this app were Figma, FigJam, Lyssna, Optimal Sort, Typeform,

My Process

Research

Research was conducted through both user surveys and user interviews.

Ideation

The self-monitoring feature has evolved from its initial two big cards into a more descriptive card, since during usability testing some users did not realise they were clickable. In the new design, both mind and body have their own cards next to the other core features: time tracking and recharge feature.

Wireframing

On the journey of building this web app, I followed an iterative design process. I started with paper sketches and improved screens along the way, by focusing on adding more complexity, depth and design principles from following the usability guidelines, to choosing colours, forms or fonts etc.

Design

Usability Testing - it helped me understand how the designs of three features are used by users and where to implement changes

Prototype

Prototype was built in Figma.
